# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Individuals, students, and professionals looking for IT mentoring, project coaching, and tech career guidance.

## Product Purpose
A landing page to showcase SobatTi's IT mentoring and coaching services, build trust, and convert visitors into students/clients.

## Brand Personality
Professional, reliable, energetic, expert, and encouraging.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS templates, overly complex or cluttered tech platforms, unprofessional or overly casual blogs.

## Design Principles
- Trust through clean structure and established design patterns
- Dynamic energy through bold accents (orange/blue)
- Clear, unmissable calls to action
- Expert confidence in typography and spacing

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility practices, maintaining high contrast ratios (especially with the brand's dark and light sections), and clear focus states.
